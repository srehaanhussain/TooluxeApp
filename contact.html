FAQ

How do I report a bug?

If you encounter any issues while using our tools, please use the contact form to report the bug. Include details such as the tool you were using, the steps to reproduce the issue, and your browser information.

Can I suggest a new tool?

Absolutely! We're always looking to expand our collection of tools. If you have a suggestion for a new tool that would be useful, please let us know through the contact form or email.

Are these tools free to use?

Yes, all tools on Tooluxe are completely free to use. We believe in providing useful utilities that are accessible to everyone without any cost.

Can I use these tools for commercial purposes?

Yes, you can use our tools for personal, educational, and limited commercial purposes, provided that such use does not violate any laws or infringe on any third-party rights. For more information, please refer to our Terms of Service.
