Our Story

Tooluxe was created with a simple mission: to provide free, accessible, and useful web tools that help people with everyday tasks. We believe that powerful utilities should be available to everyone, regardless of technical expertise or financial resources.

Our collection of tools is designed to be intuitive and straightforward, solving common problems that web users face daily. From scanning QR codes to generating secure passwords, converting text to speech, analyzing text, and converting between different units of measurement, Tooluxe aims to be your go-to resource for practical web utilities.

Our Founder

Tooluxe was founded by Rehaan Hussain. S, a passionate developer and digital entrepreneur. With a keen interest in creating practical web applications that solve real-world problems, Rehaan has developed several successful online platforms.

In addition to Tooluxe, Rehaan is also the founder of:

  • ffbuyorsell.netlify.app - A platform dedicated to buying and selling services.
  • freelancersforge.neocities.org - A community for freelancers to connect and grow.

Our Values
