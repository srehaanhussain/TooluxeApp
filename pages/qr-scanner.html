QR Code Tools

Scan QR codes or generate your own QR codes

Scan Result:

Open Link

Instructions:

  1. Click the "Start Camera" button to activate your device's camera.
  2. Point your camera at a QR code to scan it.
  3. The scan result will appear below the camera view.
  4. If the result is a URL, you can click "Open Link" to visit the website.
  5. Click "Copy" to copy the result to your clipboard.

Upload QR Code Image:

Alternatively, you can upload an image containing a QR code:

128

Instructions:

  1. Enter the content you want to encode in the QR code (text, URL, etc.).
  2. Adjust the size, color, and background color as needed.
  3. Click "Generate QR Code" to create your QR code.
  4. Use the "Download QR Code" button to save your generated QR code.
