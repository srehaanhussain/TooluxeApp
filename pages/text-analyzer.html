About Text Analysis:

Text analysis provides insights into the structure and readability of your content. This tool calculates various metrics to help you understand and improve your writing.

Readability Score is based on the Flesch-Kincaid readability tests, which estimate how difficult a text is to understand. A higher score indicates easier readability.

Lexical Density is the percentage of unique words in your text, which can indicate the richness of vocabulary used.
