Instructions:

  1. Click "Upload Image" to select an image from your device.
  2. Use the sliders to adjust various image properties:
    • Brightness: Adjust the overall lightness of the image
    • Contrast: Modify the difference between light and dark areas
    • Saturation: Change the intensity of colors
    • Blur: Add a soft blur effect to the image
    • Sharpness: Enhance the detail and clarity of the image
    • HD Quality: Increase the resolution of the image (1x to 4x)
  3. Click "Download Enhanced Image" to save your enhanced image.
  4. Use "Reset" to start over with the original image.
